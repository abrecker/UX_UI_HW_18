My UX Skills

User Research

I believe thorough research is key to efficiently and effectively solving UX/UI problems. I conduct my research through one-on-one interviews, market analysis, and company audits.

User Empathy

It is pivotal to understand one's user - their motivations, wants, needs, likes and dislikes. I develop empathy for my users by building a persona, imagining their story and mapping their experience with the product. This centers their experience at the core of the project.

Brand Building

In a world with thousands of brands trying to sell products and services, it is paramount that one's brand is unique, genuine and consistent across all platforms. Using mood boards, Canva and Figma, I develop working style tiles to build brand ethos in my projects.
